# Mechanics & Materials

This module covers the physical systems and design aspects.

## Topics
- **Statics & Dynamics**: Forces, moments, equilibrium, kinematics, kinetics.
- **Strength of Materials**: Stress, strain, beam deflection.
- **Thermodynamics & Heat Transfer**: Basic cycles, conduction, convection.
- **Fluid Mechanics**: Properties of fluids, flow dynamics, hydraulics/pneumatics.
- **CAD/CAM**: Computer-Aided Design (SolidWorks, AutoCAD) and Manufacturing.
